Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about trading, accounts, security, and more

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

▼

Creating an account is simple and takes less than 2 minutes. Click the "Create Free Account" button, enter your email address, create a strong password, and verify your email. You'll then complete our secure KYC (Know Your Customer) process by uploading a government-issued ID and proof of address. Once verified, you can deposit funds and start trading immediately.

What is the minimum deposit required?

▼

The minimum deposit varies by account type: Starter accounts require $500, Professional accounts require $5,000, and Enterprise accounts require $50,000. However, you can open an account with no minimum deposit and fund it whenever you're ready to trade.

How long does account verification take?

▼

Most accounts are verified within 24 hours. During peak periods, verification may take up to 48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your account is approved. To speed up the process, ensure all documents are clear, valid, and match the information you provided during registration.

Trading

What markets can I trade?

▼

We offer access to 500+ cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoins), 50+ forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, etc.), major stock indices (S&P 500, NASDAQ, FTSE), individual stocks, and commodities (gold, silver, oil). All markets are available 24/7 except stock markets which follow regular exchange hours.

What are your trading fees?

▼

Our fee structure is transparent and competitive: Starter accounts pay 0.2% per trade, Professional accounts pay 0.1%, and Enterprise accounts pay 0.05%. There are no deposit fees for verified accounts. Withdrawal fees vary by payment method: cryptocurrency withdrawals incur network fees, while bank transfers are free for amounts over $1,000.

Can I use leverage?

▼

Yes, we offer leverage up to 1:100 depending on the asset class and your account type. Crypto leverage is capped at 1:20, forex at 1:100, and stocks at 1:5. Please note that leverage amplifies both gains and losses. We strongly recommend understanding the risks before using leverage and utilizing stop-loss orders to manage risk.

What order types do you support?

▼

We support all major order types: Market Orders (instant execution at current price), Limit Orders (execute at specified price or better), Stop Loss (automatic sell to limit losses), Take Profit (automatic sell to lock in gains), and Trailing Stop (dynamic stop loss that follows price movement). Advanced users can also access OCO (One-Cancels-Other) and Iceberg orders.

Deposits & Withdrawals

How do I deposit funds?

▼

We accept multiple deposit methods: bank transfer (ACH/SEPA), credit/debit cards (Visa/Mastercard), and cryptocurrency transfers. To deposit, navigate to your dashboard, click "Deposit," select your preferred method, and follow the instructions. Crypto deposits are instant, card deposits process within minutes, and bank transfers take 1-3 business days.

How long do withdrawals take?

▼

Cryptocurrency withdrawals are processed within 1 hour after approval. Bank transfers (ACH/SEPA) take 2-5 business days. Card withdrawals take 3-5 business days. All withdrawals require security verification via email or SMS. For your security, first-time withdrawals to new addresses/accounts undergo a 24-hour security hold.

Are there withdrawal limits?

▼

Daily withdrawal limits depend on your verification level and account type. Basic verified accounts can withdraw up to $10,000/day, fully verified accounts up to $100,000/day, and Enterprise accounts have custom limits. If you need to withdraw more, contact our support team to request a limit increase.

Security

How do you protect my funds?

▼

We employ multiple layers of security: 98% of crypto assets are stored in offline cold wallets, all online systems use military-grade 256-bit encryption, and funds are protected by $250M insurance coverage. We also implement multi-signature wallets requiring multiple approvals for large transactions, and undergo regular third-party security audits.

Do you offer two-factor authentication?

▼

Yes, we strongly recommend enabling 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication). We support Google Authenticator, Authy, and SMS-based 2FA. Once enabled, you'll need to enter a time-based code along with your password when logging in or making withdrawals. This significantly increases account security.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?

▼

If you suspect unauthorized access, immediately change your password, enable 2FA if you haven't already, and contact our 24/7 support team. We'll temporarily freeze your account to prevent any unauthorized transactions while we investigate. Check your email for any suspicious login notifications and review your recent activity in the dashboard.

Technical Support

Is there a mobile app?

▼

Yes! Our mobile app is available for iOS and Android with full trading functionality, real-time charts, push notifications for price alerts, and biometric login. Download from the App Store or Google Play. The app syncs seamlessly with the web platform, so your watchlists and settings are always up to date.

Do you offer API access?

▼

Yes, Professional and Enterprise accounts have full REST and WebSocket API access for algorithmic trading. Our API documentation includes code examples in Python, JavaScript, and more. You can build custom trading bots, backtest strategies, and integrate with third-party tools. Rate limits apply based on account type.

How can I contact support?

▼

We offer 24/7 support via live chat (instant), email (24-hour response), and phone (+1-800-555-1234). For urgent account issues, use live chat for the fastest response. Technical questions are best handled via email where our team can provide detailed explanations and screenshots. Enterprise clients have dedicated account managers.
